Core capabilities

Admissions & Registration

Manage enquiries, applications, document checks, interviews, approvals and student onboarding.

Student Information

Academic Management

Configure academic years, classes, subjects, timetables, homework, lessons and promotions.

Exams & Report Cards

Secure by design

Protecting school data and controlling access

The platform is designed to give each user access only to the information and actions appropriate to their role.

  • Role-based access for administrators, teachers, finance, HR, parents and students
  • Secure password handling and encrypted web access
  • Audit history for critical and sensitive actions
  • Controlled approvals for results, admissions and financial adjustments
  • Validation and data-integrity controls
  • Responsive access from desktops, tablets and modern mobile browsers

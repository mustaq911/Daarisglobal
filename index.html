About Daaris Global

Built for learners, run like a system

Daaris Global is a Qatar-based company working across two connected fields: guiding students through their education journey, and building the software that helps schools manage theirs.

Every institution we work with gets the same starting point — a clear needs assessment before any commitment, so the plan fits the school rather than the other way around.

We keep both sides of the business close together on purpose. Understanding how classrooms, admissions offices, and finance teams actually work is what makes the systems we build genuinely usable.
